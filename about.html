Introduction

Chan Hon Pan, Shawn

I am a driven and results-oriented marketing professional with over 20 years of experience across Marketing, Product Management, Sales Excellence and Technical Marketing. My career has spanned the full commercial spectrum — from hands-on technical sales and acoustics consulting to leading regional business development and pricing transformation projects across Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand and India.

Currently serving as Business Development Manager at ROCKWOOL Building Materials, I am responsible for Singapore marketing and Asia-wide growth for the insulated wall panel business. I have led post-acquisition mergers, launched new product categories including agriculture growing substrates and fire protection solutions, and have been a member of the Singapore Business Management Team since 2016.

I thrive in multi-cultural, fast-paced environments and am passionate about building high-performing teams, driving commercial transformation, and identifying new market opportunities across Asia.
